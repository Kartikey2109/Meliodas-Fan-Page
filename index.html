THE DRAGON SIN MELIODAS

Meliodas「メリオダス」 is the Dragon's Sin of Wrath and captain of the Seven Deadly Sins, formerly the owner of the renowned tavern Boar Hat, and is the main protagonist of the series. His Sacred Treasure is the Demon Sword Lostvayne and his inherent power is Full Counter.

Meliodas is a demon that has lived for thousands of years. Many years in the past, he lived in the Kingdom of Danafor, and was even a knight there. However, when his lover liz was killed, Meliodas' wrath consumed him and he destroyed the entire kingdom in a rage.
